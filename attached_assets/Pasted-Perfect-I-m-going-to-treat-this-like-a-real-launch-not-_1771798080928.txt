# 4️⃣ CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT STRUCTURE (CRITICAL)

You need:

### Independent Contractor Agreement

Must include:

1. Non-solicitation (cannot contact client directly)
2. No use of your brand
3. Insurance requirement
4. Payment terms
5. Quality expectations
6. Indemnification clause
7. Dispute resolution

You are a **marketing & dispatch platform**, not employer.

DO NOT:

* Set exact hours
* Provide equipment
* Require uniform
* Control too tightly

Or you risk misclassification.
